## 摘要
现有的扩散模型在生成逼真图像方面取得了显著进展。然而，直接将这些模型应用于遥感影像往往忽略了内在的物理规律。这种忽视常常导致光谱失真和辐射不一致，严重限制了生成数据的科学应用价值。为解决这一问题，本文提出了AnyBand-Diff——一种结合光谱先验的扩散框架，专为稳健的光谱重建而设计。具体而言，我们设计了一个融合双随机掩码策略的Masked Conditional Diffusion骨干网络，使模型能够从任意波段子集恢复完整的光谱信息。随后，为确保辐射保真度，提出了一种Physics-Guided Sampling机制，利用可微物理模型的梯度显式引导去噪轨迹，使其逼近物理上合理的解空间。此外，本文还构建了Multi-Scale Physical Loss，在像素、区域及全局层面共同施加严格约束。大量实验证实，AnyBand-Diff在生成可靠影像和实现精确光谱重建方面表现卓越，推动了物理感知生成方法在地球观测领域的发展。

## Abstract
Existing diffusion models have made significant progress in generating realistic images. However, their direct adaptation to remote sensing imagery often disregards intrinsic physical laws. This oversight frequently leads to spectral distortion and radiometric inconsistency, severely limiting the scientific utility of generated data. To address this issue, this paper introduces AnyBand-Diff, a novel spectral-prior-guided diffusion framework tailored for robust spectral reconstruction. Specifically, we design a Masked Conditional Diffusion backbone integrated with a dual stochastic masking strategy, empowering the model to recover complete spectral information from arbitrary band subsets. Subsequently, to ensure radiometric fidelity, a Physics-Guided Sampling mechanism is proposed, leveraging gradients from a differentiable physical model to explicitly steer the denoising trajectory toward the manifold of physically plausible solutions. Furthermore, a Multi-Scale Physical Loss is formulated to enforce rigorous constraints across pixel, region, and global levels in a joint manner. Extensive experiments confirm the effectiveness of AnyBand-Diff in generating reliable imagery and achieving accurate spectral reconstruction, contributing to the advancement of physics-aware generative methods for Earth observation.

## 二、方法论：AnyBand-Diff 框架

### 2.1 核心思想
AnyBand-Diff 是一个**结合光谱先验与物理约束的统一扩散框架**，同时实现：
- 任意波段缺失时的光谱修复（band repair）
- 高物理一致性的遥感影像生成  

其关键创新点有三：
1. **Masked Conditional Diffusion (MCD)**：用于从任意子波段条件生成完整高光谱影像。  
2. **Physics-Guided Sampling (PGS)**：在采样/反扩散阶段引入物理梯度约束，实现采样轨迹“物理引导”。  
3. **Multi-Scale Physical Loss (MSPL)**：在像素、局部区域和全图层级引入物理一致性约束。

### 2.2 关键技术细节

#### (1) Masked Conditional Diffusion (MCD)
- **双随机掩膜（Dual Stochastic Masking, DSM）策略**：
  - 模拟不同传感器响应函数（Spectral Response Functions, SRFs）；
  - 随机掩蔽部分波段以模拟缺失数据。
- **条件自适应调制（Conditional Adaptive Modulation, CAM）机制**：
  - 编码输入掩膜与可见波段为上下文向量；
  - 在 U-Net 的不同层中通过缩放–平移参数注入条件，从而实现动态光谱特征调制。
- **优化目标**：噪声预测误差最小化  
  \[
  L_{MCD} = \mathbb{E}[\|\epsilon - \epsilon_\theta(X_t, t, C, M)\|^2]
  \]
  使网络学习从任意“不完整”观测恢复完整光谱。

#### (2) Physics-Guided Sampling (PGS)
- **思想**：在反扩散采样时引入来自物理模型 \(F_{phy}\) 的梯度，纠正生成结果中违反物理规律的偏差。
- **核心约束**：
  \[
  L^{(t)}_{phy} = \|F_{phy}(\hat X_0^{(t)}) - P_{target}\|^2
  \]
  梯度参与反扩散更新：
  \[
  \tilde\epsilon_\theta = \epsilon_\theta - s \sqrt{1-\bar\alpha_t}\nabla_{X_t}L^{(t)}_{phy}
  \]
  其中 \(s\) 为引导强度。  
  实现了基于物理梯度引导的“可微辐射传输”采样。

#### (3) Multi-Scale Physical Loss (MSPL)
- **总体目标函数**：
  \[
  L_{MSPL} = \lambda_{px}L_{pixel} + \lambda_{reg}L_{region} + \lambda_{img}L_{image}
  \]
  - **像素级（Pixel）**：约束生成的波段间相关矩阵与真实光谱一致；
  - **区域级（Region）**：利用可微物理指标（NDVI、NDWI）分布一致性；
  - **图像级（Image）**：通过简化RTM约束总体辐射一致性。  
  该分层机制可同时保持局部细节与整体物理保真。
